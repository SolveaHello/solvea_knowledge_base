# Solvea Marketing Knowledge Base

This repository is the single source of truth for Solvea's marketing content, positioning, and competitive intelligence. It is structured for use by marketing, content, and growth teams.

**Website**: https://solvea.cx

---

## Structure

### `who-we-are/`
Who Solvea is — company identity, mission, and the customers we serve.
- `overview.md` — Company description, mission, contact details, and trust/compliance credentials
- `target-audience.md` — Primary segments, industries, and buyer persona summary

### `what-we-do/`
What the product does and how it connects to the tools businesses use.
- `product-overview.md` — Full feature breakdown, supported channels, and core capabilities
- `integrations.md` — All supported integrations organized by category

### `why-us/`
Why customers choose Solvea — positioning, proof, and competitive context.
- `differentiators.md` — What genuinely sets Solvea apart in the current market (start here for messaging)
- `proof-points.md` — Customer testimonials, key statistics, and trust signals
- `competitors.md` — Categorized competitor landscape with channel matrix and threat levels

### `product-values/`
The principles behind how Solvea is built and communicated.
- `core-values.md` — Six core product values that guide features, messaging, and decisions

### `product-guidelines/`
Rules for how to talk about Solvea in any customer-facing content.
- `messaging-guidelines.md` — Brand voice, approved headlines, audience-specific messaging, and what to avoid
- `pricing-guidelines.md` — Free tier breakdown and how to frame pricing in acquisition content

### `events/`
Reserved for campaign and event-specific content.

---

## Where to Start

| Goal | Go to |
|---|---|
| Write an ad or landing page | `why-us/differentiators.md` + `product-guidelines/messaging-guidelines.md` |
| Understand the buyer | `who-we-are/target-audience.md` |
| Research a competitor | `why-us/competitors.md` |
| Describe a feature | `what-we-do/product-overview.md` |
| Check pricing copy | `product-guidelines/pricing-guidelines.md` |
| Reference a stat or testimonial | `why-us/proof-points.md` |
