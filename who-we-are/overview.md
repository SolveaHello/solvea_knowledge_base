# Who We Are

Solvea is an AI receptionist platform built for service-based businesses. We make it easy for any business — regardless of technical skill — to automate customer communications across every channel, from phone calls and SMS to email, WhatsApp, and live chat.

Our platform is built on a simple belief: every business deserves a world-class front desk experience without the overhead. Solvea puts a 24/7 AI receptionist in the hands of small businesses, agencies, and enterprises alike — no code, no setup friction, no missed customers.

## Mission

To make intelligent customer communication accessible to every business, so that no call goes unanswered, no lead goes cold, and no customer feels ignored.
