# Target Audience

Solvea's primary focus in 2026 is **service-based SMBs** — businesses where the phone still rings, bookings still get missed, and the cost of a full-time receptionist is hard to justify but the cost of a bad customer experience is even harder.

---

## Primary Portrait: The Service SMB Owner

A business owner or operator running a service-based business with 1–50 staff. They interact with customers daily through calls, messages, and bookings. They're not looking for enterprise software — they want something that works immediately, doesn't require a developer, and pays for itself in the first month.

**Who they are:**
- Owner-operators or small management teams
- Not technical — no developer on staff
- Time-poor, cost-conscious, reputation-sensitive
- Already using basic tools (Google Calendar, a CRM, maybe a booking app) but nothing that ties it all together

**What keeps them up at night:**
- Calls going to voicemail and never getting returned
- Staff turnover at the front desk
- Inconsistent responses giving customers a bad impression
- Bookings slipping through because no one was available to answer

**How they measure success:**
- Fewer missed calls and bookings
- Reduced front desk cost
- Customers who come back and refer others

## Primary Segments

- **Medical Spas & Wellness** — Appointment bookings, patient inquiries, follow-ups
- **Restaurants & Hospitality** — Reservations, guest communications, FAQs
- **Hotels & Accommodation** — 24/7 guest support, booking management
- **Law Firms** — Inbound client screening, intake, appointment scheduling
- **Barber Shops & Salons** — Calendar filling, no-show follow-ups, reminders
- **Home Services** — Lead capture, service request routing, after-hours coverage
- **Real Estate** — Lead qualification, viewing scheduling, inquiry responses

## Who We Are Not Targeting (This Year)

- **Enterprise** — Long sales cycles, procurement complexity; addressed through SLG only when inbound
- **Pure e-commerce brands** (no service component) — Different pain, different buying behaviour
- **Developers / technical buyers** — Solvea is no-code by design; this is not the right tool for builders who want API access and full control
