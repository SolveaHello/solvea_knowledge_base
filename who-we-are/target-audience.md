# Target Audience

Solvea is built for service-based businesses that deal with high volumes of inbound customer communication and need to respond faster, more consistently, and at lower cost.

## Primary Segments

- **Retail & E-commerce** — Handle order inquiries, returns, and product questions at scale
- **Medical Spas & Healthcare** — Manage appointment bookings and patient inquiries 24/7
- **Restaurants & Hospitality** — Automate reservations and guest communications
- **Real Estate** — Qualify leads and schedule viewings without lifting a finger
- **Law Firms** — Screen and respond to inbound client inquiries professionally
- **Barber Shops & Salons** — Fill calendars automatically with no-show follow-ups
- **Home Services** — Capture and route service requests around the clock
- **Software Companies** — Scale support without scaling headcount
- **Freelancers** — Maintain a professional front-desk experience on a solo budget

## Persona Summary

Our customers are typically business owners, operations managers, or customer service leads who:
- Are overwhelmed by repetitive inbound queries
- Have tried (and outgrown) manual scheduling or basic chatbots
- Want automation but don't have developer resources
- Measure success in time saved, bookings captured, and customer satisfaction
