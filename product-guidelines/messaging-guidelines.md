# Messaging Guidelines

Guidelines for how to talk about Solvea in marketing copy, ads, social posts, emails, and any customer-facing content.

## Brand Voice

- **Confident, not arrogant** — We make strong claims because we have the data to back them up. Never boastful without proof.
- **Clear, not clever** — Plain language wins. Avoid jargon, buzzwords, and AI hype. Say what Solvea does, not what it "leverages" or "empowers."
- **Direct, not pushy** — Lead with value. Let the product speak. Avoid pressure tactics.
- **Warm, not casual** — Friendly and approachable, but professional. We're a business tool, not a social app.

## Approved Taglines & Headlines

- "The most easy-to-use AI Receptionist, no-code required"
- "Live in under 3 minutes"
- "24/7 AI receptionist for your business"
- "Never miss a customer again"
- "Handle 70% of customer service — automatically"

## Key Messages by Audience

### Small Business Owners
Focus on: time savings, ease of setup, cost vs. hiring, 24/7 availability.
> "Stop losing customers to voicemail. Solvea answers every call, books appointments, and handles FAQs — automatically."

### Operations / Customer Service Managers
Focus on: automation rate, multi-channel coverage, inbox consolidation, analytics.
> "One platform. Every channel. 80% of tickets resolved without touching a queue."

### Enterprise / Brands
Focus on: consistency at scale, security certifications, CRM/helpdesk integrations, proven ROI.
> "Consistent, branded customer interactions at any volume — with SOC 2 and ISO compliance built in."

## What to Avoid

- Do not say "AI chatbot" — Solvea is a full AI receptionist (voice + multi-channel)
- Do not overpromise on automation rates without citing the source context
- Do not use generic AI buzzwords: "cutting-edge," "revolutionary," "game-changing"
- Do not omit free trial/free tier details in acquisition campaigns — it's a key differentiator

## Pricing Messaging

Always lead with the free tier when speaking to cold audiences:
- 11,000 free credits on signup
- Free 7-day phone number
- Handles up to 50 customers free
- No credit card required

This removes the #1 objection to trying a new tool.
