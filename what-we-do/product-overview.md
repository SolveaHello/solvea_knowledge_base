# What We Do

Solvea provides an AI receptionist that handles inbound customer communication across every channel — voice, SMS, email, WhatsApp, and live chat — automatically and intelligently.

Businesses configure their AI agent once (in plain language, no code required) and it goes live in under 3 minutes, ready to answer questions, capture leads, book appointments, and resolve tickets on their behalf.

## Core Capabilities

### Vibe Builder
Create and configure a custom AI agent through a conversational setup flow or by selecting a pre-built industry template. No developer required.

### Knowledge Base
Give your AI agent a brain. Upload documents, import web content, or sync data automatically so the agent always has the right answers.

### Inbox Management
All customer conversations — across every channel — land in one centralized inbox. Tickets are organized, routed, and tracked automatically.

### Contact Management
A lightweight built-in CRM that stores and organizes customer data from every interaction. No separate tool needed to keep your contacts clean.

### Deployment
Connect your AI agent directly to your website, phone number, WhatsApp, email, or any other touchpoint with one-click integrations.

### Analytics
Track performance, spot drop-off points, and get improvement recommendations — so your AI receptionist keeps getting better over time.

## Supported Channels

- Phone (voice calls)
- SMS
- Email
