# Shopify App Store — Market Overview

## Why Shopify

Solvea is entering the Shopify App Store as a PLG (product-led growth) motion after establishing enterprise credibility via SLG (sales-led growth) in e-commerce. The app store is a high-intent distribution channel: merchants are actively searching for solutions, free trials convert directly, and word-of-mouth through reviews compounds over time.

## The Opportunity

Shopify merchants are underserved specifically on **voice and multi-channel AI support**. The dominant players in the app store are helpdesk and chat tools — none of them natively handle phone calls. Solvea's full-channel stack (voice + SMS + email + WhatsApp + live chat) is a genuine gap in the current Shopify ecosystem.

## PLG Motion for Shopify

- Merchants discover via app store search or category browse
- Free tier (no card) removes signup friction
- Single-store self-serve setup — no sales call needed
- Upsell path: free → paid as ticket/call volume grows
- Reviews and ratings become the primary growth engine

## What Shopify Merchants Care About (in order)

1. **Order management** — Can the AI handle order status, refunds, and edits without human involvement?
2. **Speed of setup** — Does it work out of the box with my Shopify store?
3. **Automation rate** — What % of tickets does it actually resolve?
4. **Price vs. ticket volume** — Am I paying per seat, per ticket, or per resolution?
5. **Multi-channel** — Can it handle Instagram DMs, email, and chat from one place?
6. **AI quality** — Does it sound human? Will it embarrass my brand?
