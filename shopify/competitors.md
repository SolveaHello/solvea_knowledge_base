# Shopify App Store — Competitor Landscape

Last reviewed: March 2026

---

## Category 1: Shopify-Native Helpdesks
*Purpose-built for e-commerce support. Deep Shopify data integration is their core identity.*

### Gorgias — gorgias.com
**Positioning**: "The helpdesk built for Shopify merchants." Preferred Shopify partner.
**Channels**: Email, live chat, SMS, voice, social media
**Pricing**: $10/month (50 tickets) → $750/month (5,000 tickets). AI Agent: $0.90–$1.00 per resolved conversation. Overage at $0.32–$0.40 per 100 tickets.
**Shopify strengths**: Order management (create, edit, refund) directly from inbox. Multi-store support. Revenue statistics. 15,000+ Shopify brands. Customers: MVMT, Arc'teryx, Reebok, MrBeast.
**Weaknesses**: Expensive at scale. Per-ticket pricing punishes fast-growing merchants. No true voice-first AI agent.
**Threat level**: Very high. This is the category leader. Any Shopify merchant comparing tools will see Gorgias first.

---

### Re:amaze — reamaze.com
**Positioning**: Unified helpdesk built for online businesses and e-commerce.
**Channels**: Email, live chat, social media, SMS, VoIP, video calls, push notifications
**Pricing**: 14-day free trial; tiered (specific prices not publicly listed)
**Shopify strengths**: Native Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and Wix integrations. Real-time live dashboard. AI Agent (beta). 50+ integrations including Slack, Jira, Zapier.
**Weaknesses**: AI agent is still in beta. Less brand recognition than Gorgias. No dedicated voice AI.
**Threat level**: Medium. Strong feature set but lacks Gorgias's market dominance and social proof.

---

## Category 2: SMB Chat & AI Automation
*Broad SMB chat tools with strong Shopify presence. Not built for e-commerce but well-adopted there.*

### Tidio — tidio.com
**Positioning**: "#1 customer service app" for Shopify. AI + live chat for SMB stores.
**Channels**: Live chat, email ticketing, social platforms (no voice)
**Pricing**: Free → $29 → $59 → $749 → $2,999/month
**Shopify strengths**: 15,000+ Shopify stores, 4.6★ (1,879 reviews). Cart preview, in-inbox order management (refunds, cancellations, address changes). 40+ no-code automation templates. Lyro AI: 67% automation rate.
**Weaknesses**: No voice support. Chat/text only. E-commerce focused — less suited for service-business logic like booking.
**Threat level**: High for chat-only use cases. Direct competitor in the SMB + free-tier segment.

---

## Category 3: Enterprise Helpdesks with Shopify Integration
*Dominant in mid-market and enterprise. Shopify integration exists but is not their core identity.*

### Intercom — intercom.com
**Positioning**: #1 AI agent and next-gen helpdesk. Enterprise-first.
**Channels**: Web chat, email, help center (limited voice)
**Shopify fit**: Integration available but not a Shopify-native product. Complex and expensive for most merchants.
**Threat level**: Low in the app store. Overkill for most Shopify merchants.

### Zendesk — zendesk.com
**Positioning**: Enterprise-grade support platform.
**Channels**: Email, chat, voice, social, messaging
**Shopify fit**: Shopify integration via marketplace but not built for it. Per-seat pricing is steep for SMBs.
**Threat level**: Low in the app store. Used by larger DTC brands but rarely by independent merchants.

## Solvea's Gap in This Market

No app in the Shopify ecosystem currently offers **voice AI + full digital channels (email, WhatsApp, SMS, chat) in a single no-code tool** with a free tier. Gorgias is the dominant force but is text-heavy and per-ticket expensive. Tidio owns the free/SMB chat segment but has no voice. Solvea's entry point is merchants who are losing customers on the phone while their chat tool handles text.
