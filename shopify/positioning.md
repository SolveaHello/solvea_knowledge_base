# Solvea on Shopify — Positioning & Messaging

## Positioning Statement

For Shopify merchants who are losing customers to unanswered calls and fragmented support channels, Solvea is the only AI receptionist that handles voice, SMS, email, WhatsApp, and live chat from a single no-code app — so every customer gets a response, on every channel, without adding headcount.

---

## What to Lead With in the App Store

### Headline options
- "Answer every customer — on every channel. Automatically."
- "The AI receptionist Shopify merchants were missing — now handles calls too."
- "Voice + chat + email + WhatsApp. One AI. No code. Free to start."

### Key proof points for Shopify audience
- 80% resolution rate for retail interactions
- 100% call answer rate (medspa — applicable to any appointment-driven store)
- Trusted by Anker and Dreame — brands Shopify merchants recognize
- Free tier: 50 customers handled, no card required

---

## Messaging by Use Case

### Order inquiries & status
> "Customers ask 'where's my order?' dozens of times a day. Solvea handles it automatically — pulling from your store data and responding via phone, SMS, or chat, whichever channel they reach out on."

### Appointment & booking (for service-adjacent stores)
> "For merchants who also take bookings — wellness, beauty, home services — Solvea handles scheduling across every channel without a separate booking tool."

### After-hours coverage
> "Your store never sleeps. Neither does Solvea. Capture every lead and answer every question, even at 2am."

### Reducing support workload
> "Stop routing 70% of your support tickets manually. Solvea resolves them automatically and escalates only what needs a human."

---

## Shopify-Specific Differentiator

Unlike Gorgias (per-ticket pricing that gets expensive fast) and Tidio (chat only, no voice), Solvea:
- Covers voice — the channel no Shopify app currently owns
- Credits-based pricing scales more predictably than per-ticket models
- Works for merchants who sell AND take service appointments (hybrid businesses)
